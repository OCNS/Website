CNS*2019 Barcelona: Workshops Program

This is the list of workshops to be held at CNS*2019. This page will be updated as more information becomes available. Please check back regularly for updates.

Workshops are either 1.5 days, 1 day, or 0.5 days long, and are distributed into three 0.5 day sessions:

  • Session 1: Tuesday 16 July, morning session: 0930 - 1310.
  • Session 2: Wednesday 17 July, morning session: 0930 - 1310.
  • Session 3: Wednesday 17 July, afternoon session: 1450 - 1830.

For inquiries related to these workshops, please contact the workshop coordinator at workshops@cnsorg.org, or the individual workshop organizers listed below.
