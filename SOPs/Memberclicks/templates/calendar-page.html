CNS*2019 Barcelona, Spain: Calendar

The CNS*2019 event calendar can be viewed below. Please click each event for further information. The calendar is still being updated with new information. Please check back regularly.

The calendar above can also be viewed here in a separate page: https://tinyurl.com/cns-2019-calendar. Google Calendar users can add this calendar to their personal accounts by clicking the "+" sign in its bottom right corner. They can also copy individual events to their personal accounts by clicking on an event, and clicking "Copy to my calendar". Users of other calendar services may subscribe to this calendar by using this link to access the ICS file. Once subscribed, any updates made to this calendar should also reflect on personal devices.
